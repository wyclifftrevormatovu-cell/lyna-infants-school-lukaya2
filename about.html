Who We Are

Lyna Infants’ Day and Boarding Primary School Lukaya is a privately ran school located in Agip Cell Central Ward Lukaya TC Kalungu District in Uganda. The school with the help and support of the community started under a CBO (Lyna Community Foundation) which later registered as Lyna Children`s Foundation, whose primary purpose is to touch lives and build futures of Children of low income earners and those from un well to do families and those who were affected directly or indirectly by HIV scouge that greatly affected the people of Lukaya and Masaka as well as Uganda in general. The school has a wide curriculum intended to bring up holistic children and we have always performed well academically justifeid by the consistent supper performance in national final exams since our inception. Lyna Infants’ Day and Boarding Primary School is a disciplined and child-friendly learning institution committed to providing quality education that nurtures academic excellence, good morals, talents, and essential life skills.

The school offers both day and boarding education in a safe, caring, and supportive environment that promotes holistic child development.

Our Vision

To become the most successful school in literacy, numeracy, morals, and skills, producing holistic learners prepared for self-sustainability.

Our Mission

To provide basic and quality education through dedicated teaching, strong values, and co-curricular activities that empower learners for self-reliance and a joyful future.
