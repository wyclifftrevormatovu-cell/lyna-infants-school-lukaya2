Admission Information

Lyna Infants’ Day and Boarding Primary School welcomes learners from all backgrounds into a disciplined, caring, and supportive learning environment. Admissions are conducted in a transparent and parent-friendly manner.

Classes Offered

Nursery Section

Baby Class, Middle Class and Top Class with a strong foundation in early learning, play-based activities, and moral development.

Primary Section

Primary One to Primary Seven following the national curriculum with emphasis on literacy, numeracy, science, co-curricular activities, and life skills.

Day & Boarding

We offer both day and boarding options. Boarding learners are provided with proper accommodation, balanced meals, close supervision, and a safe environment.

Admission Requirements

Parents or guardians are requested to present the following:

• Copy of birth certificate or baptism card
• Previous school report (where applicable)
• Two passport-size photographs
• Medical information (for boarding pupils)

How to Apply

1. Visit the school premises at Lukaya near Road Toll Market.
2. Meet the school administration for guidance.
3. Fill in the admission form.
4. Submit the required documents.
5. Receive confirmation upon successful assessment.
